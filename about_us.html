About Leon Steels: Building Trust Since 2020

Leon Steels Private Limited was incorporated on 29th October 2020, backed by 18+ years of expertise in the steel and cement distribution industry in Chennai. Since our inception, we have remained committed to quality, transparent pricing, and reliable delivery across the Chennai–Chengalpattu belt.

What We Offer

TMT Bars

Available in 8mm, 10mm, 12mm, 16mm, 20mm, 25mm, 32mm in premium brands like ARS, Tata, Pulkit, MCR as well as reliable local brands, ensuring high tensile strength for your structural needs.

Cements

All grades available from UltraTech, Dalmia, Coramandel, Chettinad, ensuring durability for your slabs, columns, and masonry work.

Concrete Blocks & Precast Beams

Solid blocks, hollow blocks (2-hole and 3-hole), column blocks of various sizes, and lintel beams are available for compound walls, load-bearing structures, and gates.

Blue Metal, M-Sand, P-Sand

Quality aggregates for your concrete mix needs.

  • Precision sizing for faster construction
