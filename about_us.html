About Leon Steels: Building Trust Since 2020

Leon Steels Private Limited was incorporated on 29th October 2020, backed by 18+ years of expertise in the steel and cement distribution industry in Chennai. Since our inception, we have remained committed to quality, transparent pricing, and reliable delivery across the Chennai–Chengalpattu belt.

About Leon Steels

Leon Steels Private Limited, founded by Lourdes Monica with guidance from Sir Soris Barnapas, brings over a decade of entrepreneurial experience to the construction sector. Focused on distributing high-quality TMT rebars, cement, and cement products, Leon Steels partners with leading brands like ARS, Kamatchi, and TATA Tiscon to deliver reliable materials at competitive prices. The company actively engages with masons and industry influencers to address on-ground needs while supporting dealers and retailers in a competitive market. Operating from a large facility in Chengalpattu, Leon Steels has achieved significant growth, expanding its reach to meet the evolving demands of Chennai’s construction industry while maintaining strong partnerships and service excellence.
